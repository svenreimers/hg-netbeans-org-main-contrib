OPC application. The order processing center (OPC) is at the heart of the adventure builder enterprise, responsible for coordinating all activities necessary to fulfil a customer order. It interacts with all the other applications through web services. It exposes three different web services that are accessed by the other applications:
  • an order submitting service
  • an order tracking service
  • a centralized broker service for interactions with suppliers

It also acts as a client and accesses the bank's web service and the suppliers' web services.
